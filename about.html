This project is dedicated to the internet phenomena Bowsette! She is the original work of thousands of people on the internet- an icon born from the hivemind of internet Nintendo fan after the release of new footage for an upcoming Mario video game. I found the fact that the development of her popularity and lore was completely a result of grassroots media campaigns with no corporate involvement to be inspiring. This, combined with my love for her aesthetic, resulted in this fanpage!
