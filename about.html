This project is dedicated to one of the anime world's most important couples: Haruka Tenoh and Michiru Kaiou! Both are apart of the Sailor Moon franchise and serve as members of the Outer Senshi, or Soldiers of the Outer Solar System. Beyond their impressive prowess in the battlefield, however, lies a kindredness in spirit unique to these two women alone. By themselves they are a solemn violinist and passionate wanderer; together, they form a cohesive team capable of overcoming any obstacle.
